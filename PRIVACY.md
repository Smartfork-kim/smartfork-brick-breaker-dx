# Privacy Policy

SMARTFORK Brick Breaker DX runs locally in your browser.

- No account is required.
- No personal information is collected by the game itself.
- The game stores the best score in browser localStorage on the user's device.
- If hosted on a third-party platform such as GitHub Pages or itch.io, that platform may process basic access logs according to its own policy.

Contact: https://smartfork.kr/
